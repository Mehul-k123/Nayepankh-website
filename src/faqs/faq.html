Nayepankh foundation was initiated to bring a change and help people during the tough times of Covid. Later in the year, the NGO started to expand their operations and provide help to a wider section of the society.

As we all know, 2020 was a year the world was striving to survive the global pandemic of covid-19. During these dire times we felt an urge to make an impact with whatever we had, and so we tried!! We tried to uplift the underprivileged and help the needy with all our resources and material we could arrange.
